The problem is that Framer Motion's animations are adding transform properties which are breaking the fixed positioning!

âœ… The Real Fix
The issue is that Framer Motion's motion.div is applying transforms during animation, which creates a new containing block.

Here's the solution - we need to separate the Portal wrapper from the animated content:
